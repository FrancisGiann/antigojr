Our Story

We are four students from STI College Lucena — Francis, Kurt, Khurt, and JB. As part of our school project, we created this website to showcase the Wariza Family Antique Business. The Wariza family has been collecting and selling antiques for many years. Each item holds a story and connects us to the past. We saw the value in sharing these treasures online so more people can see and appreciate them. This website is not just a project — it's our way of helping a local business grow and keep its legacy alive. Through this, we learned a lot about teamwork, coding, and design. It was a fun and meaningful journey for all of us. We hope you enjoy exploring the website as much as we enjoyed building it. Thank you for visiting, and welcome to the world of Wariza Antiques!
